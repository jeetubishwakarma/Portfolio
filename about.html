About me

I am a Web Designer and Video Editor and currently, I am on my way to become a Full Stack Developer from Udacity and Skill Sigma Online. I have been using Adobe Softwares since 2012 and completed many courses from online learning platforms such as Lynda, PluralSight, and Udemy. Computer Science is always been my favorite subject in School and College so I started coding JavaScript and begin to learn Web Designing and Development.


When it comes to designing and editing graphics and videos Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Dimension, Affinity Designer, and Affinity Photo are my most favorite computer software I spend most of the time working with this software. As I said I am a Web Designer so coding is something that moves me a lot so Dart for Flutter and JavaScript are my favorite programming languages I like Designing and Developing UI and UX with these languages.

I completed my 10 + 2 study in 2017 and dropped out in the 2nd (2019) year of my college.
